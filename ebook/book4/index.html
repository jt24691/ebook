Jeep’s first name was G.P. which stands for “general purpose” which was designated by the army.
When they began to sell them to the public, they were so used to being called G.P.s that they called them Jeeps instead.
The first Jeep was called the Willy it served from WW2 to the Korean war.
Then other models like the Commanders, Liberty, Renegade, Patriot, and the newer truck.
